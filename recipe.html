About

Welcome to the White Sauce Pasta Recipe page! Here, you'll find a simple and delicious recipe for making creamy white sauce pasta at home. Perfect for family dinners, quick meals, or special occasions.

Follow our easy steps and enjoy the rich, cheesy, and satisfying flavors of this classic dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed herbs and chili flakes (optional)
  • Vegetables (like bell peppers, corn, and broccoli - optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add all-purpose flour. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Gradually add milk, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Add grated cheese and stir until smooth and creamy.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and optional herbs or chili flakes.
  6. Add cooked pasta and vegetables (if using) to the sauce and mix well.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your white sauce pasta!
